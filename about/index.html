About

Jaz-Marie is a concatination of my first and middle names, Jazmine + Marie, and is a place for me to show who I am and what I do. I'm always learning; this website was created out of a desire to learn web development and a need to motivate myself to the end. What better way to motivate yourself other than to make it real? So I sketched out what I wanted, regardless of how little I knew, and I got to work learning what I needed to know in order to make it happen.

This site is by no means perfect or complete. It is, and will be (for now), a work in progress. And because life is about more than just code, I wanted to also show a glimpse into who I am when I step away from the computer screen. I love writing code, and I also love photography, yoga, traveling, and various other things.
