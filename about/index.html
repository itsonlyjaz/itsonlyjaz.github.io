About

Jaz-Marie is a concatination of my first and middle names, Jazmine + Marie, and is a place for me to show who I am and what I do. I'm always learning -- this website was created out of a desire to learn web development and a need to motivate myself to the end. What better way to motivate yourself other than to make it real? So I sketched out what I wanted, regardless of how little I knew, and I got to work learning what I needed to know in order to make it happen.



I studied C in school, use Java at work, and am interested in a few other languages like Scala and Python. So the big question is why? I work with power regulators and it's really interesting learning how they work and observing their behavior. But coding allows for much more flexibility and I love being able to turn a concept in my head into something that I can present visually to others. HTML, CSS, and Javascript are powerful little tools with many, many branches such as Node.js and AngularJS (both of which, I'm currently learning!). The list of languages and scripts can go on and on and that, for me, is a big part of what draws me to them: you will never get to a place where your learning is complete.



Needless to say, this site is by no means perfect or complete. It is, and will be (for now), a work in progress. And because life is about more than just code, I wanted to also show a glimpse into who I am when I step away from the computer screen. I love writing code, and I also love photography, yoga, traveling, and various other things.
